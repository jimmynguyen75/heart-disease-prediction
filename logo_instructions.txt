INSTRUCTIONS FOR ADDING THE UNIVERSITY LOGO
===========================================

To add the HCMC International University logo to your application:

1. Save the university logo image file as: logo.png
   
2. Place it in this directory:
   /Users/jimmy/Desktop/Thesis Project/Project 2/logo.png

3. The logo will automatically appear in:
   - The Streamlit web application (top of the page)
   - The generated PDF reports (title page)

LOGO SPECIFICATIONS:
- Format: PNG (recommended) or JPG
- Recommended size: At least 500x500 pixels
- The app will automatically resize it to fit

NOTE: If the logo.png file is not found, the application will still work
but will display only the text without the logo image.
